This is Manish elaganti,for me,Music expresses feeling and thought, without language.It was below and before speech, and it is above and beyond all words.i listen to lot of music in my free time, I list my favorite albums and most listened. Music cleanses the understanding; inspires it, and lifts it into a realm which it would not reach if it were left to itself. A quiet secluded life in the country, with the possibility of being useful to people to whom it is easy to do good, and who are not accustomed to have it done to them; then work which one hopes may be of some use; then rest, nature, books, music, love for one’s neighbor — such is my idea of happiness.
